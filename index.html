The Problem

Construction sites leave a hidden waste crisis

Cranes and heavy machinery clear the big stuff. But what's left microplastics, metal shards, cigarette butts is too small to see from a distance, too spread out to pick by hand, and too persistent to ignore.

Our Solution

An autonomous robot built for the last mile of site cleanup

After big debris is cleared, NinjaBot navigates autonomously, collects all remaining micro-waste, empties itself, and returns to charge no human needed.
